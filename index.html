About Us

Shaping the Leaders of Tomorrow

Rajan International Academy is a premier CBSE-affiliated English medium co-educational institution located in Basti, Uttar Pradesh. Founded with a vision to deliver globally competitive education, the academy offers a nurturing environment that combines academic rigour with holistic personality development — from Playgroup right through Class 12.

🎯 Our Vision

To be the most trusted centre of learning in Purvanchal that shapes confident, compassionate, and globally competent citizens.

🚀 Our Mission

To provide quality education through innovative pedagogy, value-based learning, and strong character formation from an early age.

Academics

A Curriculum Built for the Future

Following the CBSE curriculum, our academic programme balances conceptual learning with creative thinking and real-world problem-solving skills.

📚

CBSE Curriculum

Aligned with the National Education Policy, our CBSE programme emphasises deep understanding over rote learning. Students develop analytical skills, critical thinking, and a strong conceptual foundation across all subjects.

🌟

Holistic Development

Beyond academics, students participate in cultural programmes, sports, debates, science exhibitions, and art — developing leadership, teamwork, and communication skills that prepare them for real-world success.

🔬

Practical Learning

Hands-on experiments in well-equipped labs, project-based assignments, and interactive sessions ensure students don't just learn — they understand, apply, and innovate.

💻

Digital Literacy

Our Computer Lab and technology-integrated classrooms equip students with digital skills essential for the 21st century, from basic computing to research and digital communication.

🎨

Creative Arts

Music, drawing, and craft are integral to our curriculum. We believe creativity is a foundational skill, and our dedicated arts programme helps students express themselves and think innovatively.

🏅

Sports & Physical Education

Regular physical education, playground activities, and inter-school sports competitions keep students fit, foster team spirit, and teach them the values of discipline and perseverance.
